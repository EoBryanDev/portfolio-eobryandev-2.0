## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Social and Contact Links
The system SHALL provide a footer with links to social media (LinkedIn, GitHub) and a direct contact CTA.

#### Scenario: Clicking LinkedIn link
- **WHEN** the user clicks the LinkedIn link in the footer
- **THEN** they are redirected to the user's LinkedIn profile

### Requirement: No Resume Download
The system SHALL NOT provide a direct PDF download of a resume.

#### Scenario: Seeking resume
- **WHEN** the user looks for a resume
- **THEN** they only find links to professional profiles (e.g., LinkedIn)
